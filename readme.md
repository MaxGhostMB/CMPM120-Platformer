Player Mechanics:
    Movement: W, A, S, D
    Jump: Space
    Walljump: Hold to cling to the wall with A or D and jump
    Dash: Shift + A, Shift + D
    Ground Pound: Shift + S

lvl 1: the tutorial, this level showcases double jumps and wall climbing - made by Both
lvl 2: showcases dashing - Will
lvl 3: has a lock and key puzzle and moving platforms - Max

level select:
pushing the 1, 2, 3 keys takes you to each corresponding level. 4 takes you to the secret level

Secret Level:
in lvl 1 there is a secret door that takes you straight to lvl 3 and also has a small key to access the gem for a multi-level puzzle, the way to get there is to jump up past the first door. 
in lvl 3 continuing up towards the vines takes you to the secret level where you can collect the secret gem, if collect it will appear on a pedestal at the end 

EC included:
Double jumping and wall jumping
Alternate player movement
Ways for player to die/be incapacitated
Puzzles, lock-and-key puzzle
Dynamic level Elements
Secret levels
Moving platforms
