Player Mechanics:
    Movement: W, A, S, D
    Jump: Space
    Walljump: Hold to cling to the wall with A or D and jump
    Dash: Shift + A, Shift + D
    Ground Pound: Shift + S

lvl 1: the tutorial, this level showcases double jumps and wall climbing 
lvl 2: showcases dashing
lvl 3: has a lock and key puzzle and moving platforms
